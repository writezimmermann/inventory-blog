---
title: 'Book review - Electrolyte in Blue'
date: '2024-04-22'
---

Electrolyte in Blue by Jim Dine is one of those books I flipped through at the bookstore
and had to have it. Theres something about Jim Dines mixed media style of drawing and commenting
his work with chalk and paint that I really resonate with. The book is published by Steidl so the 
quality is absolutely top notch. Highly recommended!
